There are a couple of different ways to submit your pitch:

  • iPhone: All recent iPhones have a built in app called Voice Memos. Open the app and record your pitch. You'll be able to listen to your recording, edit, delete, and re-record. When you're happy with it, press Done, save your voice memo, and email it straight from your iPhone to community@vpr.net.

  • Email: Record your pitch using any audio software, then send it in an email to community@vpr.net.

  • Voicemail: Prepare your thirty second pitch, find a quiet spot, then leave it in a voicemail at 1-802-552-4880. You won't be able to edit or listen to it, so make sure you're ready when you call.
