There are a few different ways to submit your pitch:

  • Voicemail: Prepare your pitch, and when you're ready, call 802-555-5555 to leave it in a voicemail.
  • SoundCloud: Record your pitch and post it to SoundCloud, then email the link to community@vpr.net.
  • iPhone: Use your iPhone's build in Voice Memos app, then email the audio file to community@vpr.net.
  • Email: Record your pitch using any audio software, then email the MP3 to community@vpr.net.
